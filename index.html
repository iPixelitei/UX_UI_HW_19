DuoQ Moblie Case Study

DuoQ was made to originally help gamers achieve better synergy in their gameplays by being paired up with fellow teammates with similar styles. Through our testing we discovered a majority of players prefer to keep a private and anonymous public profile and will only open up to those they feel comfortable with. While our original plan still stands, we added options and features to help our users keep private. This has helped us understand our target audience and was a key factor to shaping our app.

AbelGamers Non Profit Case Study

The Ablegamers Charity is a non-profit organization created to tackle social isolation and cultivate an inclusive community for people with disabilities.In this case study we focused on making it easy to donate and seeing the stories of people AbleGamers have helped, however, looking forward we think it would be beneficial to look over the format they have for contacting them.
